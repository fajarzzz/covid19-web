Covid 19 Protocols & Basic Protective Measures

Stay At Home

Don't leave the house if there is no urgent need.

Use Mask

Wear the mask properly wherever you go.

Wash Your Hands

Regularly wash your hands with with soap and water.

Maintain Social Distancing
Maintain at least 1 metre distance between yourself and anyone.
